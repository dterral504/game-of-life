Welcome to the Game of Life

Instructions

  • You will first be prompted to enter the number of rows and columns that the board will contain. Enter your answer in integer form.
    • Example: 9
  • Next, you will be prompted to enter the initial state of each row on the board. Enter a string of 1's and 0's separated by spaces.
    • Example: 1 0 0 1 1 1 0 1 0
  • Your results can be seen in the console!

Click 'Start' to begin!
